Caregiving

Caregiving is for us to express our culture of care, allowing for our peers to be able to drink and have fun in a safe environment. We are dedicated to giving care to those who might need it by giving them water, giving them food, providing a safe space to recuperate, and calling REMS if the situation requires it.
