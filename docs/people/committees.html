Arts

The Arts Committee is at the forefront of creatively connecting and engaging our community in a variety of ways. Currently, our members can embark on projects such as our birthday banners that are posted in the Yurt; help host events like “Painting with a Twist” and other collabs; create posters, designs, and any artistic needs for other committees; and oversee McGallery registration and displays.

If you’re looking for a way to flourish with your artistic skills, then this is the place for you—beyond just sharing what you love, you’ll have the chance to learn from members about many other art forms. Whether it’s music, dance, painting, theater, or more, we’re always open to bring more creativity to Murt! If you don’t see your chosen artistic passion in our current projects, we’re always happy to help new projects grow. :)
